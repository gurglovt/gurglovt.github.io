This is not my dog.

I found this image a long time ago, maybe in 2020. I don't know whose dog it is or even where I originally found it.

The image was so funny to me that I got it put on a black tshirt. The tshirt is now very old and the black has faded to a sort of grey, and the image of the dog has been scraped away and turned into a sort of industrial-style abstract expressionist art piece (10cm x 6cm on cotton).

You can click the image if you want to see.

I wonder how much it will fade in total, or if, after enough washes, it will eventually become white -- or whatever the true colour is... beneath...

I have recurrent thoughts

I frequently think about...

  • the impossible overlapping of the mind, body, and the soul
  • the mind and soul as parasites to the body
  • this dog

...and I'm not really sure why that is, but I'm not going to beat myself up over it. :)
